About Insura

Insura is a platform designed to give people a clear, complete overview of their insurance. Today, most policies are scattered across PDFs, emails, and different portals, making it difficult to understand coverage or identify gaps. As a result, many people either overpay for years or remain underinsured where it matters most.

Insura solves this by automatically reading and structuring uploaded insurance documents, turning complex policies into a simple, easy-to-understand overview. The platform also benchmarks coverage, helping users instantly see if they are overpaying, missing protection, or unnecessarily double insured.

When improvements are needed, users can request better offers from insurers with a single action — no forms or hassle. A subscription layer adds continuous monitoring, ensuring coverage and pricing stay optimal over time, so users never have to actively manage their insurance again.

In addition, Insura includes a secure "family vault," making important insurance information accessible to the right people in case of emergencies.

For users
Subscription for ongoing monitoring and peace of mind — so your coverage and pricing stay optimal automatically.
For insurers
Commission model when users actively request better offers — aligned incentives, no cold outreach.
